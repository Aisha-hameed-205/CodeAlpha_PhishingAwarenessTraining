# **Phishing Attack Report**

## **Introduction**

Phishing is a cyber attack in which attackers trick users into revealing sensitive information such as usernames, passwords, bank details, or personal data by pretending to be a trusted source through emails, messages, or fake websites.

## **How It Works**

1. The attacker sends a fake email or message.
2. The victim clicks a malicious link or attachment.
3. The victim enters personal information on a fake website.
4. The attacker steals the information.

## **Types of Phishing**

* Email Phishing
* Spear Phishing
* Whaling
* Smishing
* Vishing

## **Effects**

* Identity theft
* Financial loss
* Data breach
* Malware infection

## **Prevention**

* Do not click suspicious links.
* Verify the sender's email.
* Use strong passwords and Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA).
* Keep your software updated.
* Never share personal information through unknown websites or emails.

## **Conclusion**

Phishing is a serious cybersecurity threat that targets human trust. Staying alert, verifying suspicious messages, and following basic security practices can help protect users from phishing attacks.
